Sushi Project

Zuzu Sushi wanted me to create an online ordering platform for home delivery.
the goal was to provide a user-friendly experience where customers can easily select sushi options and input their delivery address, for all sushi lovers.

Features

Important things you can do include seeing when your order will arrive right on the main page, finding out when we're open and how to reach us,

Picking out your favorite sushi from a big assortment, entering your address, and checking your delivery details and confirmation.

Technologies

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap
  • Backend: PHP
  • Database: MySQL
