Fitness Project

Training Factory, een sportschool, vroeg ons om een platform te maken voor diverse groepslessen zoals vechtsporten, krachttraining en cardio. Leden moesten flexibel per les kunnen betalen. Het belangrijkste was het scheppen van een vriendelijke sfeer waarin zowel beginners als ervaren sporters zich welkom voelen. Het doel was niet alleen gevarieerde fitnessopties aanbieden, maar ook nieuwe leden aantrekken door een positieve sportervaring te bieden.

Clients Vision

We want customers to be able to use our website easily, where they can easily register, have an overview of group classes, sign up for each class, pay, and unsubscribe.

Trainers can create and manage their own classes with time and date, with insight into registrations and payments.

For the admin, there is a clear management system to manage sports categories and have an overview of all members and trainers, providing complete control of Training Factory.

User Roles

Visitor
On the website, visitors can discover what Training Factory stands for, view the class offerings, and learn more about different sports. They can easily find opening hours and contact information.

Members
As members, they can sign up, pay, and unsubscribe per class, and easily manage their personal information.

Trainers
Trainers at Training Factory can create and manage their classes, including time and date. They have insight into registrations and payments, allowing them to efficiently plan and manage their classes.

Admin
Admins at Training Factory have a clear management system. They can manage sport categories and have an overview of all members and trainers.

Technologies

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Twig
  • Backend: PHP, Symfony
  • Database: MySQL
